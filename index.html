Computer Vision for Agriculture Hackathon
Makerere University
22 - 26 April 2024
Code. Create. Innovate.
The hackathon will focus on building machine learning solutions based on datasets Maize dataset and Coffee dataset that were collected by the Makerere AI Lab as part of the Lacuna Project.

The Hackathon will target university students (undergraduate and graduate) who will be mentored and compete in teams to build machine learning solutions around some of the selected datasets
